Rajat Shukla

1 week ago

A Journalist turned Explorer, who travelled 405 days nonstop without using any money.

We are talking about a person who is a Journalist, Explorer, World record holder and an Entertainer.

He is Rajat Shukla who started his career as a journalist and covered general, crime and entertainment beat with some prestigious organizations. Interviewed famous Bollywood celebrities like Akshay Kumar, John Abraham, Kangana Ranaut, Sunny Leone, Neil Nitin Mukesh, Bachchan Family and Telugu industry actors among many others.

He also interviewed OTT actors like Pankaj Tripathi, Abhishek Banerjee, Amit Sial, etc.

A long time of his career was also into interviewing tv celebrities that included Kapil Sharma and team, Karan Patel, Heena Khan, Shaheer Shaikh, Rupali Ganguly, Remo D'Souza & team and almost every lead actor of daily soaps and reality tv shows.

Nowadays YouTube and Instagram are also creating celebrities that people love to follow. He had interviewed Flying beast, Sejal Kumar, Radhika Bangia, Awez Darbar, Team 07 and many more amongst them.

Towards the end of the year 2020, He took a break and started hitchhiking towards Nepal on an unplanned trip that was supposed to be only in the areas around Mumbai.

He came back to India and visited every state and UT for 405 days. The twist in the story is, He did all this without using money and the way he managed his food, stay and travel became an attraction for many media channels like The Better India, Zee News, Radio City 91.1, Red FM 93.5 and many national and regional newspapers.

The life of a nomad has now become his identity and this is what he loves to do and hence he keeps promoting travel by all means.

He has continued pushing his limits and is keen to achieve new goals. One of it is his world record for completing the hike of 9058 meters by climbing 82,000 steps up and down in 26 hours. 150 repetitions of a hill called Baronda in Virar near Mumbai.

He loves to talk, meet new people and explore different places while also hosting lot of Corporate and Cultural ceremonies as an Anchor and a Performer. He has hosted shows of well known brands like Decathlon, Mobil, Futsal premier league amongst many others.

Continuing to be a journalist and also an adventurer he keeps motivating people to travel through his startup www.travelvj.com

You should visit it once to know how big and beautiful our world is and how short and limited our life is.

Keep travelling, Keep exploring, Keep smiling.
