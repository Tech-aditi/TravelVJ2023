Have stories to tell, not stuff to show.

Let's pay attention on few very important decisions we make in our lives.
Are we just earning to pay interior designers, builders, caterers and decorators ?
Whom do we want to impress with our highly inflated house properties & fat weddings ?

Relationships these days are unpredictable, it used to someday end with our lives but now its seen ending in the court rooms.

We don't own properties, we just have temporary name on documents. GOD laughs sarcastically, when someone says, "I am the owner of this land" !

Why are we working like dogs and cats in our prime years of life ? How many generations do we want to feed ? How much is the "need" and how much do we actually "want" ? Think about it.

Would our next generation be incapable to earn, that we save so much for them ? Can not we spare atleast one day a week for friends, family and self ? Do you spend even 5% of your monthly income for your self enjoyment ? Usually...No.

Why can't we enjoy simultaneously while we earn ? Spare time to enjoy before you have slipped discs and cholesterol blocks in your heart !

Do not judge a person only by the length of his car.
Many of our science and maths teachers were great personalities riding on scooters !
It is not bad to be rich, but it is very unfair, to be only rich.

Let's get a LIFE, before life gets us, instead....
Better late then never, get up and start exploring this beautiful world.
Life is short and the world is big.
You will take nothing but just these experiences along when you are gone.
